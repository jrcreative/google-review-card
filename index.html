Step 1: Add your content

  1. Get the link from your Google Business Profile here and paste it into the field.
  2. Be sure to test the QR code with your phone to make sure it works before printing it.

Step 2: Choose your layout

3 1/2in x 2in - Horizontal Layout

Great for business cards, stickers or magnets. Leave them behind with clients, or provide each technician or service person with one and have them ask customers to scan at the time that they're most satisfied with your service or product.

2in x 3 1/2in - Vertical Layout

Great for vertical business cards, stickers and lanyards. Lanyards can be worn by technicians and service workers who can request reviews at time of completion or payment when the customer is most satisfied.

5in x 7in - Table Top Stand

Great for restaurants, bars, and retail locations where people remain and have the time to scan and leave their review.
